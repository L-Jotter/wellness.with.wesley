General Information and Qualifications.

You can expect a calm, intentional, "slow-and-steady" sessions, conducted virtually, allowing you to participate from anywhere. Minimal equipment required. You will be able to hear and see the instructor. And he can see you to provide encouragement and instruction.

Proven Experience

Formerly a Lifestyle Coach for the YMCA of the Inland NW, I spent 2.5 years as a personal trainer and group facilitator specializing in active older adults and chronic disease management.

Real Results

I have successfully helped clients lower their HbA1c, regain the ability to get up off the floor independently, and significantly reduce joint pain.

Educational Foundation

I hold a B.S. in Nutrition and Exercise Physiology, providing a clinical understanding of how the body moves and heals.
